Balancing Tutorial

Half-Reaction Method:

  1. Identify: Write the oxidation and reduction half-reactions.
  2. Balance Atoms: Balance elements other than O and H.
  3. Balance Oxygen: Add H₂O to balance O.
  4. Balance Hydrogen: Add H⁺ to balance H (acidic medium).
  5. Balance Charge: Add electrons (e⁻) to equal the charge.
  6. Multiply: Multiply half-reactions by integers so electrons lost = electrons gained.
  7. Add: Sum the equations and cancel out common substances.
💡 Pro-Tip

In easy levels, focus on matching the total charge of the left side (reactants) with the right side (products).
